SQAI-NCTS Workshop on Quantum Technologies and Machine Learning

August 25-29, 2025
National Taiwan University, Taipei, Taiwan

About

This workshop focuses on the related topics of tensor network and its potential application in the wide research field such as the quantum algorithms. Topics covered by it include the quantum embedding, quantum machine learning, condensed matter problems, etc.
